## Why Choose AI Voice-Over?

Creating voice-overs for short videos is one of the biggest pain points for content creators. Recording yourself? Not professional enough. Hiring a voice actor? Expensive and slow.

With AI voice-over tools, you just type your script and get professional-quality audio in 30 seconds. This tutorial uses **EchoVoice** as an example.

---

## Step 1: Prepare Your Script

Good voice-over starts with a good script. Keep these tips in mind:

- **Keep it concise**: 50-150 words per segment works best
- **Write conversationally**: Use spoken language, not formal writing
- **Use punctuation**: Commas and periods control the AI's pacing

**Example script**:

> "Have you ever wondered why some short videos grab your attention from the first word? The secret is voice-over. Today I'll show you how to create one in 30 seconds."

## Step 2: Choose Your Voice Style

Open [EchoVoice Web Trial](https://echovoiceai.net/en/try). You'll see three control dimensions:

### Voice Role
- **Male**: Great for business, documentary style
- **Female**: Perfect for lifestyle, beauty content
- **Warm Male**: Ideal for emotional, healing content
- **Youthful**: Suits energetic, fun content

### Tone
- **Natural**: Everyday conversational feel — the universal choice
- **Broadcast**: News, formal presentations
- **Deep**: Mystery, storytelling
- **Bright**: Energetic, sports content

### Emotion
- **Calm**: Works with most scenarios
- **Happy**: Product reviews, recommendations
- **Sad**: Story-telling, emotional content

> 💡 **Tip**: Not sure what combination to use? Start with "Female + Natural + Calm" — it's the most versatile combo.

3. Click **"Generate & Play"**
4. Listen to the result — if satisfied, export via the Mac app
