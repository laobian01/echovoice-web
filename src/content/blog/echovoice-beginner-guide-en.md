## Quick Start Guide

### Step 1: Open the Trial Page

Visit [echovoiceai.net/en/try](https://echovoiceai.net/en/try).

### Step 2: Sign In

Click the **"Sign In"** button in the top-right corner. Create an account with your email. You'll automatically receive 5 free credits.

### Step 3: Choose Your Voice Style

In the trial panel, you'll see three dropdowns:
- **Role**: Choose the voice type
- **Tone**: Choose the speaking style
- **Emotion**: Choose the emotional color

### Step 4: Enter Your Script

Type or paste your text into the script box. Start with a short script (30-80 words) to test the effect.

### Step 5: Generate and Listen

Click **"Generate & Play"** and wait a few seconds. Your AI voice-over is ready!
